## 1. Cart & Checkout Flow (Highest Priority)

These turn browsing into purchases.

1. **Cart Page**

   * View selected products
   * Update quantity / remove items
   * Show subtotal, taxes, shipping estimate

2. **Checkout Page**
   Often split into steps:

   * Shipping address
   * Delivery method
   * Payment details
   * Order review

3. **Order Confirmation / Success Page**

   * Order number
   * Summary of purchased items
   * Estimated delivery date

---

## 2. Authentication & User Account

Needed for real-world usage.

4. **Login Page**

5. **Sign Up / Register Page**

6. **Forgot Password / Reset Password**

7. **User Profile / Account Dashboard**

   * Personal info
   * Saved addresses
   * Saved payment methods

8. **Order History Page**

   * List of past orders
   * Link to order details

9. **Order Details Page**

   * Status (processing, shipped, delivered)
   * Tracking info

---

## 3. Search & Discovery Enhancements

10. **Search Results Page**
11. **Category Pages**

12. **Filter / Sort Results**

* Price, rating, brand, availability

13. **Wishlist / Favorites Page**
